PIXEL PENGUIN

🐧

WINTER RUNNER

Help our brave penguin navigate through the frozen Antarctic landscape! Jump over icy obstacles and see how far you can waddle!

HOW TO PLAY

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Help the pixel penguin run as far as possible through the Antarctic wasteland! Avoid the dangerous ice spikes and collect points to set a new high score.

🎮 CONTROLS

SPACEBAR
Jump
CLICK/TAP
Jump

🧊 OBSTACLES

Ice Spikes (0-99 pts): Sharp crystalline spikes on the ground
Snowballs (100-199 pts): Rolling snowballs to jump over
Icicles (200-299 pts): Hanging icicles floating in the air
Ice Blocks (300-399 pts): Large frozen blocks
Frozen Fish (400+ pts): Slippery frozen fish floating around

New obstacle types appear every 100 points! Each has different sizes and positions.

📊 SCORING

• Earn 1 point every second you survive
• Earn 10 bonus points for each obstacle you pass
• Game speed increases over time for extra challenge
• Your high score is automatically saved!

🔊 SOUND EFFECTS

Enjoy immersive 8-bit style sound effects:
• Jump sounds when penguin leaps
• Score chimes when passing obstacles
• Level-up fanfare every 100 points
• Game over sound when you crash

🌨️ WINTER FEATURES

Experience the beautiful Antarctic environment with falling snow, moving clouds, and icy terrain. New obstacle types unlock as you progress!
